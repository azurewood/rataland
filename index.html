About

New Chinatown is currently an efficient service platform dedicated to creating innovative services for the Chinese people! We provide products and services such as yellow page advertisements, flea markets, rentals, cross-border consignment, job recruitment, etc. For landlords, New Chinatown is a permanent free advertising distribution channel, an easy-to-use property management tool that facilitates top-level updates, and an efficient platform that attracts tenants and allows your high-quality properties to be instantly exposed. For new international students and immigrants, it is a house-hunting website with a large number of housing listings covering a wide area. The second-hand market can provide everyone with a platform to choose cheap and practical daily necessities.

Currently supporting New Zealand and Australia, we also welcome people of insight from other countries to join us to discuss cooperation and create a better tomorrow. New Chinatown, your warm home in the southern hemisphere! stay tuned!
